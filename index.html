Bio

I am currently a developer student at DeltaV Code School in Cedar Rapids, IA! I am enjoying greatly and cannot wait for the opportunity to utilize what I am learning.

I was born in Illinois while my father was in the navy. I spend the next few decades moving from state to state, most in the Midwest. Iowa seemed to be the most common destination, so I decided to just settle here! I enjoy exploring and hiking in the woods with my two dogs, taking long drives in my Kia, and reading a good sci-fi/fantasy book.


Goals

My current goal is finishing the DeltaV program with the best grades I can get! After that I hope to find a job as a Software Developer, so I can take advantage of all the programming I have learned. I would also like to work on different apps to help people with ADHD.

I am also training towards a 5k trail race!
